1. Information We Collect

a) Account Information (Login & Registration)

When you create an account or log in, we may collect information that you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (if provided)
  • Login credentials

This information is required to create and manage your user account.

b) Device & Usage Information

The app may automatically collect limited technical information such as:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App performance data and crash reports
  • Network connection status

This data helps us improve app performance and reliability.

2. Media Access Permission

The application requests permission to access photos and media files (READ_MEDIA_IMAGES) only when you choose to upload or select an image within the app.

  • Media access occurs only after user action.
  • The app does not scan or access files in the background.
  • No media files are accessed without your permission.

3. Internet Usage

The app uses internet access to:

  • Authenticate login and registration
  • Communicate with secure servers
  • Load application content
  • Download files requested by users

4. File Downloads

The application may download files requested by users using a secure download service. Downloaded files are saved locally on your device and are not shared without your interaction.

5. How We Use Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. Information may be processed only by trusted service providers required to operate the application (such as hosting or analytics services).

7. Data Retention

We retain your account information only as long as your account remains active or as required to comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your account at any time.

8. User Rights & Control

You may:

  • Update or delete your account information

We implement reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect your information. However, no electronic storage method is completely secure.
