03

Account Registration & Login

CABA APP's landing page is publicly accessible. However, access to all services — including chat and other interactive features — requires a registered account.

Why registration is required: Account creation ensures that only verified users can access sensitive services, protecting both you and other users of the platform from unauthorized access and abuse.

What we collect at registration

When you register, we collect your email address and a password. Your password is never stored in plain text — it is encrypted using industry-standard hashing algorithms. You may optionally provide a display name or profile picture.

Authentication

Login sessions are managed using secure tokens. We do not store your credentials in an unprotected manner on your device. You may log out at any time, which invalidates your session token.

04

Internet & Network Access

CABA APP requests the following network permissions:

  • INTERNET — Required to communicate with our servers, load content, and enable real-time features such as chat.
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE — Used to detect your current connectivity (Wi-Fi vs mobile data) so the app can adapt gracefully to network changes.

The app is configured with usesCleartextTraffic="true" to support certain internal network environments. All sensitive data transmissions use HTTPS/TLS encryption regardless of this setting.

05

File & Image Uploads

CABA APP allows registered users to upload files and images within the app (e.g., inside chat or service features). To enable this, the app requests the READ_MEDIA_IMAGES permission on Android 13 and above.

What you can upload

Content TypeWhere UsedStored On Server?
Images (JPG, PNG, etc.) Chat messages, profile, service requests Yes — until deleted
Documents (PDF, etc.) Service-related submissions Yes — until deleted
Other files As supported by specific features Depends on feature

How uploaded content is handled

  • Files are uploaded only when you explicitly choose to share them — no automatic background uploads occur
  • Uploaded files are stored on our secure servers and are accessible only to you and the intended recipient(s) within the app
  • We do not use uploaded content for advertising, training AI models, or any purpose beyond delivering the service
  • You may request deletion of your uploaded files at any time by contacting us or using in-app deletion features

Your responsibility: Please do not upload files containing sensitive personal information (e.g., Aadhaar, PAN, financial records) unless required for a specific service, and only through the designated secure upload feature.

Permission usage

The READ_MEDIA_IMAGES permission is used solely to open your device's gallery so you can select an image to upload. We do not scan, index, or access any photos beyond what you explicitly select.

06

File & Image Downloads

CABA APP includes a download feature (powered by Flutter Downloader) that allows you to save files and images shared within the app directly to your device.

What you can download

Content TypeSaved ToSynced to Server?
Images (JPG, PNG, etc.) Device local storage / gallery No
Documents (PDF, etc.) Device Downloads folder No
Other files Device local storage No

How downloads work

  • Downloads are triggered only by your explicit action (tapping a download button)
  • Download progress and completion status are tracked locally on your device only — this information is never sent to our servers
  • Files are saved using Android's FileProvider API, which provides scoped, secure access without exposing your broader file system to the app
  • Once a file is saved to your device, it is under your control; we have no further access to it

Managing downloaded files

Downloaded files remain on your device until you manually delete them. CABA APP does not maintain any record of what you have downloaded after the transfer is complete. You can manage downloaded files through your device's built-in file manager or gallery app.

Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. Specifically:

  • Account data is retained until you request deletion of your account
  • Uploaded files and images are retained on our servers until you delete them or delete your account
  • Usage logs are retained for up to 12 months for debugging and security purposes
  • Deleted account data and associated uploaded files are purged from our systems within 30 days

Locally downloaded files remain on your device until you delete them manually — we have no control over files once saved to your device.

Security
